FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find fast answers to common questions about treatment, locations, and pricing.

Alignora clear aligners are designed for comfort. While you may feel a mild pressure or snugness during the first 1-2 days of a new aligner set, it is virtually pain-free compared to traditional metal braces. This pressure simply means your teeth are moving into their planned positions.
The average treatment time is around 6 months, though this depends entirely on case complexity. Minor spacing or crowding (Lite plan) can take as little as 4-5 months, while more complex alignments (Premium plan) may take 9-12 months.
Yes! One of the biggest benefits of Alignora aligners is that they are fully removable. You should remove them before eating or drinking anything other than cool water. This means no food restrictions, and you can brush and floss normally before putting them back in.
Yes, clear aligners are an excellent option for teenagers whose permanent teeth have fully erupted. Our specialist orthodontists (Dr. Naufal and Dr. Elbin) will assess teen suitability during the initial 3D digital scan.
Yes, wearing retainers is essential to keep your teeth in their new positions. Alignora includes free retainers with both our Lite and Premium plans to ensure your beautiful new smile lasts a lifetime.
We offer flexible payment plans starting at very affordable rates. During your consultation, our team will provide a customized financing plan that fits your budget.
